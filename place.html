About Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country located in Southern Africa. It is known for its diverse wildlife and stunning landscapes, including the iconic Victoria Falls, one of the largest and most famous waterfalls in the world.

The country boasts a rich cultural heritage, with numerous ethnic groups and languages, making it a vibrant place to explore.
