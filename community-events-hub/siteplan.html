Website Planning Document

Site Name

Community Events Hub

This name reflects a central location for community events, promoting social gatherings and activities in the area.

Optional domain availability: communityeventshub.org

Site Purpose

The site serves as a hub for local community events, providing information on upcoming activities, registration forms, and volunteer opportunities.

Scenarios

  • What events are happening this weekend?
  • How can I volunteer for community activities?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #4a90e2 (used for headings and buttons)

Secondary Color: #ffcc00 (used for accents and highlights)

Typography

Headings: Nunito Sans (used for all headings)

Body: Open Sans (used for paragraph text)
