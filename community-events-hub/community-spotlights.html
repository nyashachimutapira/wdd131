The Local Food Pantry

Serving the community since 2010

The Local Food Pantry has been a cornerstone of our community for over a decade, providing essential food supplies to families in need. What started as a small church initiative has grown into a community-wide effort serving over 500 families each month.

Their mission: "No one in our community should go to bed hungry." Through partnerships with local farms, grocery stores, and volunteers, they distribute fresh produce, pantry staples, and prepared meals.

Get involved: Volunteer opportunities are available every Tuesday and Thursday. Food donations can be dropped off at their Main Street location.

Christalia Shumba: Volunteer of the Year

Community Champion since 2018

This year's Volunteer of the Year award goes to Christalia Shumba, whose tireless efforts have made a significant impact across multiple community organizations.

Christalia dedicates over 20 hours each week to various causes including the Food Pantry, Youth Mentorship Program, and Community Garden Initiative. Her "Clean Streets Project" mobilized over 200 volunteers last year to beautify neighborhood streets.

"I believe in the power of community," says Christalia. "When we come together, we can create real change right where we live."
